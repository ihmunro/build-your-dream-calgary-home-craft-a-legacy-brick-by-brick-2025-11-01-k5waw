About Calgary Dream Homes

Founded in 2008, Calgary Dream Homes emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the custom home building experience in Alberta. Our founder, James Patterson, recognized that homeowners deserved a builder who combined artistic vision with meticulous craftsmanship. What started as a small team of passionate builders has grown into an award-winning firm recognized throughout Western Canada for excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. Over the past 15+ years, we've transformed hundreds of dreams into reality, earning the trust and loyalty of families across Calgary and beyond.

Our core mission is to build homes that inspire, endure, and enhance the lives of those who inhabit them. We believe that a home is more than just a structure—it's a reflection of who you are and what you value. We're committed to sustainable practices that respect our environment, cutting-edge technology that enhances daily living, and uncompromising quality in every detail. Our team of architects, engineers, and craftspeople share a common passion: creating homes that exceed expectations and stand the test of time. We measure our success not by the number of homes we build, but by the lasting relationships we forge and the joy our clients experience in their new homes.
