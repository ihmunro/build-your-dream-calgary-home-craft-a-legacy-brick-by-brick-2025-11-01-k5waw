About Calgary Dream Homes

Founded in 2008, Calgary Dream Homes emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the custom home building experience in Alberta. Our founder, James Patterson, recognized that homeowners deserved a builder who combined artistic vision with meticulous craftsmanship.

What started as a small team of passionate builders has grown into an award-winning firm recognized throughout Western Canada for excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. Over the past 15+ years, we've transformed hundreds of dreams into reality.
